Alun Alun Malang

Jl. Merdeka Selatan, Kiduldalem, Kec. Klojen, Kota Malang, Jawa Timur 65119

Destination Overview

Alun Alun Malang is very well known by all Indonesian people, especially for Malang residents themselves. Visiting the city of Malang will not taste good if you don't take the time to stop by the main square first. There are many exciting activities and historical values ​​stored in this Malang City Square. This place can be the starting point for your exciting journey to visit various tourist destinations in Malang.
